# Optionality

## Definition

Optionality is the value of keeping future paths available.

## Why It Matters

In dealmaking, Naval emphasizes that contracts, investors, and partnerships can close down future options. Because the world offers many possible paths, one should be careful about constraining the future for a merely acceptable deal.

## Key Points

- Deals are long-term and can be hard to unwind.
- Contracts trade future freedom for a shared cooperative project.
- Suboptimal deals can damage companies, founders, and partnerships.
- Preserving time, peace, reputation, and strategic flexibility can be more valuable than closing a deal quickly.
