# Stag Hunt

## Definition

A Stag Hunt is a coordination game where individuals can pursue small rewards alone or cooperate to pursue a larger shared reward.

## Why It Matters

Naval uses Stag Hunt as a model for society, startups, contracts, and high-trust collaboration. Unlike Prisoner's Dilemma, the main problem is not betrayal under interrogation; it is whether people can trust each other enough to coordinate on larger upside.

## Key Points

- High-trust societies make larger cooperative projects possible.
- Low-trust environments push people toward smaller, safer, less ambitious efforts.
- Contracts can be understood as agreeing how to split the stag while giving up rabbit-chasing options.
- Small, high-competence teams can pursue outcomes that individuals could not achieve alone.
