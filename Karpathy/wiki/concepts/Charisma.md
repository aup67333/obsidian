# Charisma

## Definition

In [[wiki/sources/Sell the Truth|Sell the Truth]], charisma is defined as projecting confidence and love at the same time, or power plus good intentions.

## Why It Matters

This definition connects truthfulness with emotional delivery. Honesty is the bedrock, but if honesty is delivered without kindness, people may stop listening. Charisma is therefore not just charm; it is effective truth delivery.

## Key Points

- Confidence without care can become harsh or domineering.
- Kindness without honesty can become ineffective or false.
- Truth plus kindness helps advice become actionable rather than merely correct.
- The same empathy that makes communication effective can make hard actions, such as firing, emotionally difficult.
