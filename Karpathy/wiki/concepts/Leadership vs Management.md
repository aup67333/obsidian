# Leadership vs Management

## Definition

Management tells people what to do. Leadership makes people want to do it.

## Why It Matters

Naval connects selling to leadership: both require genuine motivation, not fake pressure. To lead well, one must understand what people want, where their capabilities fit, and how the mission overlaps with their own objectives.

## Key Points

- Leadership cannot be faked; the motivator has to be real.
- Good recruiting sells the mission by connecting it to autonomy, capability, and personal desire.
- Startups are framed as attractive to high-agency people because they offer freedom and direct impact.
- A taste of freedom can make people less employable in traditional structures.
