# Power Law Returns

## Definition

Power Law Returns describe environments where the largest winner can be worth more than many subsequent winners combined.

## Why It Matters

In [[wiki/sources/Sell the Truth|Sell the Truth]], power-law returns explain why investors and founders care about grand-slam outcomes rather than merely good early exits.

## Key Points

- The best outcome can dominate the rest of the portfolio.
- This makes preserving exposure to large upside strategically important.
- Power-law logic reinforces [[wiki/concepts/Nonlinear Returns|Nonlinear Returns]] and [[wiki/concepts/Optionality|Optionality]].
