# Antoine de Saint-Exupery

## Identity

Antoine de Saint-Exupery is referenced in [[wiki/sources/Sell the Truth|Sell the Truth]] for a leadership idea: do not merely assign shipbuilding tasks; create longing for the sea.

## Relevance

The reference supports [[wiki/concepts/Leadership vs Management|Leadership vs Management]]: leadership works by awakening desire and shared purpose, not merely issuing instructions.
