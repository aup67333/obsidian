# Naval Ravikant

## Identity

Naval Ravikant is the main speaker in [[wiki/sources/Sell the Truth|Sell the Truth]]. In this source, he is presented as a gifted communicator and seller, though he rejects the frame that he is using sales technique.

## Relevance

Naval's model of persuasion centers on credibility, honesty, clear explanation, and genuine excitement. He links sales to leadership, recruiting, dealmaking, optionality, trust, and personal motivation.
