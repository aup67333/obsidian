# Robert Cialdini

## Identity

Robert Cialdini is referenced in [[wiki/sources/Sell the Truth|Sell the Truth]] as the author associated with the CLASSR influence framework: consistency, liking, authority, scarcity, social proof, and reciprocity.

## Relevance

Naval treats Cialdini's framework as useful but secondary to motivation, credibility, empathy, and genuine belief in what is being sold.
