# Nonlinear Returns Favor Upside Over Small Spoils

## Statement

In power-law environments, it is often better to preserve future upside than to fight over small present spoils.

## Evidence For

- [[wiki/sources/Sell the Truth|Sell the Truth]] argues that technology and investing can produce 100x or 1,000x upside.
- The source says time and optionality should be reserved for opportunities with large asymmetric returns.
- It treats reputation, peace, and future openness as valuable alongside money.

## Evidence Against Or Tensions

- The source acknowledges that sometimes one must fight on principle or to avoid being taken advantage of in the future.
- It does not provide a precise threshold for when a dispute is too small to pursue.

## Current Assessment

Supported as a heuristic for venture/startup contexts, with an explicit exception for deterrence, principle, and mental peace.
